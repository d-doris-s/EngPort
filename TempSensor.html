Temperature Sensor

September 2024 - December 2024

This temperature sensor warns the user when the temperature is out of the desirable range from 70°F to 75°F. I modeled the components and designed a 9V battery holder using Onshape. The holder is 3-D printed in a vertical orientation with a zigzag infill to ensure that the arms are flexible enough for the battery to slide in while the base and walls are rigid. I programmed the Arduino for analog-to-digital conversion and component operation including the buzzer and the LED. The circuit was soldered together and battery holder was super-glued in place.
