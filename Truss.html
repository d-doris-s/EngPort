Truss Design

September 2024 - December 2024

Designed and optimized a truss structure to maximize load capacity while maintaining a budget under $300, using a MATLAB algorithm for force analysis and iterative improvement. The final design met expectations, achieving a 76.95% increase in load capacity from the initial design and supporting 1.6 kg, while also improving the load-to-cost ratio to 0.2794 oz/$ from 0.1579 oz/$ initially. Efficient material selection and cost management left one-third of the budget unused, indicating potential for further optimization.
